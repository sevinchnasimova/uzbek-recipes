discover uzbek foods

background

Uzbek food is meat-heavy, but there are dishes that everyone can love. The national Uzbek dish is plov, which is rice prepared with meat, onions and carrots.

tea

Many south-west regions in Uzbekistan, including Samarkand and Bukhara love to drink green tea because it can cool people down during hot temperatures. Many people drink their tea in pialas, which are small bowls, and they usually fill them up half-way because it makes the tea cool down faster.

bread

Uzbek bread, or non, is eaten with almost everything.

Every region has it's own style of non and they are often baked in a hot clay oven known as tandyr.
